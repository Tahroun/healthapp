About

This ‘Healthy Lifestyle’ hybrid application has been developed by Tahroun Bissonauth(170186) and Irfaan Nubee(170196).

The aim of this app is to raise awareness concerning people’s health and well-being, and lastly, promote a healthy lifestyle.

Lifestyle is referred to the characteristics of inhabitants of a region in special time and place. It includes day-to-day behaviors and functions of individuals in job, activities, fun and diet.

A healthy lifestyle is one, which helps to keep and improve people’s health and well-being. According to WHO, 60% of related factors to individual health and quality of life are correlated to lifestyle. Today, wide changes have occurred in life of all people. Obesity, physical inactivity, unhealthy diet, tobacco smoking, risky alcohol consumption, high blood pressure and high cholesterol are aspects of an unhealthy lifestyle.

With the help of this app, people will know what kind of meals they should opt for breakfast, lunch and dinner whether they are non-vegetarian or vegetarian. Not only diet plans are provided, but also workout plans for several body parts will be shown in this app and location for fitness centers in Mauritius.

ABDOMINAL CRUNCHES

ABDOMINAL CRUNCHES x 22


Lie on your back with your knees bent and your arms behind your head.
Then lift your upper body off the floor. Hold for a fer seconds and slowly return.
It primarily works the rectus abdominis muscle and the obliques.

SIT UPS

SIT UPS x 22


Lie on your back with your knees bent and your arms behind your ears.
Then lift your upper body off the floor to the sitting position.Dont tug your neg when you get up.
Slowly go back to the start position and repeat the exercise.
If your spine hurts, please stop and change to another workout plan.

JUMPING JACKS

Jumping Jacks x 30


Start with your feet together and your arms by your sides, then jump up with your feet apart and your hands overhead.
Return to the start position then do the next rep. This exercise provides a full-body workout and works all your large muscle groups

PLANK

Plank 35s


Lie on the floor with your toes and forearms on the ground.
Keep your body straight and hold this position as long as you can.
This exercise strengthens the abdomen, back and shoulders.

PUSH-UPS

Push-Ups x 10


Lay prone on the ground with arms suppporting your body.
Keep your body straight while raising and lowering your body with your arms.

Curtsy Lunges

CURTSY LUNGES x 28, each side x14


Stand straight up. Then step back with your left leg to the right, and bend your your knees at the same time.

PLIE SQUATS x 16


Stand with your hands on your hips and feet a little wider than shoulder width apart.
Then lower your body until your thighs are parallel to the floor.
